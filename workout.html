Your Weekly Training Split

A structured plan for balanced muscle growth and recovery.

Upper Body Foundation

A comprehensive stimulus for all major upper body muscles.

Barbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps (2-3 min rest)
Bent-Over Barbell Row: 3 sets of 6-8 reps (2-3 min rest)
Seated Dumbbell Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (1-2 min rest)
Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip): 3 sets of 8-10 reps (1-2 min rest)
Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 2 sets of 12-15 reps (1 min rest)
Triceps Pushdowns: 2 sets of 12-15 reps (1 min rest)

Lower Body Power

Heavy, compound movements to build foundational leg strength.

Barbell Back Squat: 4 sets of 6-8 reps (2-3 min rest)
Romanian Deadlift (RDL): 3 sets of 8-10 reps (2 min rest)
Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg (1-2 min rest)
Seated Leg Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1-2 min rest)
Standing Calf Raises: 4 sets of 10-15 reps (1 min rest)

Rest & Active Recovery

Allow muscles to repair and replenish energy stores.

Focus on Active Recovery:

Engage in 20-45 minutes of low-intensity activity to aid recovery. This helps increase blood flow, reduce soreness, and prepare your body for the next workout.

  • Brisk Walking
  • Light Cycling or Swimming
  • Gentle Yoga or Stretching

Push Dominance

Develop the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (1-2 min rest)
Flat Machine Chest Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1-2 min rest)
Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (1 min rest)
Dips (Chest Version): 3 sets to failure (2 min rest)
EZ-Bar Skull Crushers: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1 min rest)

Pull Strength

Build back width and thickness, plus biceps.

Pull-Ups (or Assisted/Lat Pulldown): 3 sets of 6-10 reps (2-3 min rest)
T-Bar Row: 3 sets of 8-10 reps (2 min rest)
Seated Cable Row (Neutral Grip): 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1-2 min rest)
Face Pulls: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (1 min rest)
Preacher Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1 min rest)

Leg Hypertrophy

Focus on volume and metabolic stress for muscle growth.

Leg Press: 4 sets of 10-15 reps (1-2 min rest)
Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg (1-2 min rest)
Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (1 min rest)
Lying Leg Curls: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (1 min rest)
Seated Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps (1 min rest)

Rest & Recharge

Final recovery day to prepare for the week ahead.

Prepare for the Week:

Use this day for complete rest or light active recovery. Focus on proper nutrition and hydration to maximize the benefits of your training week and ensure you're ready for Monday.

  • Full Rest
  • Light Stretching
  • Meal Preparation
